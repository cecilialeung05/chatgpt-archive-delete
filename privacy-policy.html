# Privacy Policy - ChatGPT Multi-Select Archive & Delete Manager ## Introduction The ChatGPT Multi-Select Archive & Delete Manager is a Chrome Extension designed to help users efficiently manage their ChatGPT conversation history. ## Data Collection This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, messages, or other user information. All operations are performed locally within the user's browser. ## Permissions The extension requests permission to interact with `https://chatgpt.com/` for the sole purpose of allowing users to select, archive, and delete conversations. No other permissions are requested or required. ## Data Usage - The extension does not store data outside of the user's browser. - No personal information is transmitted or shared with third parties. ## Changes to This Policy Any changes to this policy will be updated on this page. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically. ## Contact For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue on the official GitHub repository: [ChatGPT Archive Delete](https://github.com/cecilialeung05/chatgpt-archive-delete). Effective Date: March 20, 2025
